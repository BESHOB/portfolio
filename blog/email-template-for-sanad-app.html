Email Template for SANAD App

July 18, 2026 • 5 min read

Launching a new application is more than publishing it to the App Store or Google Play—it starts with introducing users to the platform in a clear, engaging, and professional way. For SANAD, a smart Kuwaiti platform connecting customers with nearby businesses and service providers, I designed and developed a bilingual launch email campaign that showcased the app's purpose, benefits, and early achievements.

The project combined graphic design, email development, and usability, resulting in a production-ready email template that works seamlessly across major email providers.

Project Goals

The objective was to create an email that would make a strong first impression while remaining compatible with the wide variety of email clients used by subscribers.

Bilingual Support

To reach SANAD's diverse audience, the campaign was developed in two complete versions.Included Languages:

- English

- Arabic (RTL)

Rather than relying on automatic translation, each version was individually structured to provide a natural reading experience while respecting language direction and typography.

Key Features

The template was designed not only for recipients, but also for the team responsible for sending it.

Email Provider Friendly

The HTML structure follows email development best practices, making it compatible with most popular email clients.This helps ensure consistent rendering across platforms while reducing formatting issues.

Easy Content Management

The template was organized into reusable sections, making future updates simple without requiring major code changes.

One-Click Copy Function

One of the most useful features built into the project is a Copy Template button. Instead of manually selecting the email content, the button automatically:

- Selects the fully rendered email inside the browser.

- Copies the rendered content to the clipboard.

- Allows marketers to simply paste it directly into their email service provider.

This workflow dramatically reduces preparation time and eliminates accidental formatting mistakes during campaign creation.
